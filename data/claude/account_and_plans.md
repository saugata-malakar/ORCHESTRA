# Claude Account Management & Plans

## Get Started with Claude
Claude is Anthropic's AI assistant available at claude.ai. You can access Claude through:
- claude.ai (web)
- Claude mobile apps (iOS, Android)
- Claude Desktop (macOS, Windows)
- Claude API and Console
- Claude Code (CLI tool)
- Amazon Bedrock
- Google Cloud Vertex AI

## Account Management

### Logging In
- Log in at claude.ai with your email or Google/Apple account.
- If you see a login error, try: disable VPN, disable browser extensions, clear browser cache and cookies.
- Check status.claude.com for active incidents.

### How to Get Support
- Free users: Submit requests through the help center.
- Pro/Max users: Priority support available.
- Team/Enterprise: Dedicated support channels.
- For billing issues, account access, or technical problems, visit support.claude.com.

### Change Email Address
Contact Anthropic support to change the email associated with your Claude account.

### Delete Your Claude Account
1. Log in to claude.ai.
2. Go to Settings.
3. Select "Delete Account."
4. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion.
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and removes all conversations, projects, and data.

### Export Your Data
You can export your Claude data:
1. Go to Settings > Privacy.
2. Click "Export Data."
3. Download the export file.

### Log Out of All Sessions
1. Go to Settings > Security.
2. Click "Log out of all sessions."
This will sign you out on all devices.

## Choosing a Claude Plan

### Free Plan
- Access to Claude with usage limits.
- Basic features available.

### Pro Plan ($20/month)
- Higher usage limits.
- Priority access during high-traffic periods.
- Access to Claude's latest models.
- Extended thinking capability.
- Claude Code access.

### Max Plan ($100/month)
- Highest usage limits (20x more than Pro).
- All Pro features included.
- Extended context window.

### Team Plan ($30/user/month, min 5 users)
- Everything in Pro.
- Team workspace for collaboration.
- Admin controls and user management.
- Centralized billing.
- Conversations are not used for model training by default.

### Enterprise Plan
- Custom pricing.
- SSO/SAML integration.
- SCIM provisioning.
- Advanced admin controls.
- Dedicated support.
- Custom data retention policies.

## Team and Enterprise Workspace Management

### Adding and Removing Team Members
- Admins can add members through Settings > Members.
- Invite by email address.
- Remove members by clicking the three-dot menu next to their name and selecting "Remove."

### Workspace Access
- Team workspace is managed by workspace owners and admins.
- If you lose access to a team workspace, contact your workspace admin.
- Only workspace owners and admins can restore seat access.
- Anthropic support cannot directly restore workspace access — the workspace admin must re-add you.

### Roles in Team/Enterprise
- **Owner**: Full control, billing, and member management.
- **Admin**: Member management and workspace settings.
- **Member**: Standard access to the workspace.

## Usage Limits and Errors

### Usage Limit Best Practices
- Usage resets every 5 hours.
- If you hit your limit, wait for the reset or upgrade your plan.
- Use shorter, focused conversations for better efficiency.
- Start new conversations for unrelated topics.

### Troubleshoot Error Messages
- "5-hour limit reached": Wait for reset or upgrade plan.
- "Capacity constraints": Temporary high demand. Try again in a few minutes. Not an outage.
- "Length limit error": Message too long. Break into smaller chunks or start new conversation.
- Login errors: Disable VPN, clear cache, check status.claude.com.

### Extra Usage
- Paid plan users can enable extra usage in Usage settings.
- Extra usage is billed based on consumption beyond plan limits.
