# HackerRank Account Settings & User Management

## User Account Settings and Preferences

### Update or Reset Password
To update your password:
1. Log in to your HackerRank account.
2. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner.
3. Select Settings.
4. Go to the Security section.
5. Enter your current password and new password.
6. Click Save.

To reset a forgotten password:
1. Go to the HackerRank login page.
2. Click "Forgot your password?"
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Follow the email instructions to set a new password.

Note: If you signed up using Google login, you do not have a separate HackerRank password. To set one, use the "Forgot your password?" flow with your Google email.

### Delete Your Account
To delete your HackerRank account:
1. Log in to your HackerRank account.
2. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select Settings.
3. Scroll to the Delete Account section.
4. Click Delete Account and follow the prompts, entering your password to confirm.

Warning: Deleting your account will permanently remove all data and cannot be undone.

If you signed up via Google login and don't have a password:
1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot your password?"
2. Enter the email linked to your Google login.
3. Follow instructions to set a new password.
4. Then proceed with account deletion using the new password.

### Change Email Address
Contact HackerRank support to change the email associated with your account. This cannot be done self-service.

## Teams Management

### Adding Users to a Team
1. Go to Settings > Teams.
2. Click Create Team or select an existing team.
3. Click Add Members.
4. Enter the email addresses of team members.
5. Assign roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer).
6. Click Add.

### Removing Users from a Team / Platform
To remove a user from the HackerRank platform:
1. Go to Settings > Users.
2. Find the user you want to remove.
3. Click the three-dot menu next to their name.
4. Select Remove User.
5. Confirm the removal.

Note: Removing a user revokes their access to all tests, interviews, and team data. Their historical data (created tests, reports) is preserved.

### User Roles
- **Owner**: Full access to all features, billing, and settings.
- **Admin**: Can manage users, teams, tests, and interviews. Cannot manage billing.
- **Recruiter**: Can create tests, invite candidates, and view reports.
- **Interviewer**: Can conduct interviews and view interview reports.
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to tests and reports.

To change a user's role:
1. Go to Settings > Users.
2. Click on the user.
3. Select the new role from the dropdown.
4. Click Save.

### Removing an Interviewer
To remove an interviewer from the platform:
1. Go to Settings > Users.
2. Find the interviewer.
3. Click the three-dot menu (three dots) next to their name.
4. Select Remove User or Deactivate.
5. Confirm the action.

Note: If you do not see the remove option, you may not have Admin permissions. Contact your account administrator.

## Company Level Admin Settings

### Subscription Management
- View your current plan and usage under Settings > Subscription.
- To upgrade or downgrade, contact your HackerRank account manager or sales team.
- To pause a subscription, contact HackerRank support. Self-service pause is not available.
- To cancel, contact your account manager.

### Security Settings
- Enable SSO (Single Sign-On) for your organization.
- Configure IP restrictions for admin access.
- Set password policies for team members.

### Infosec and Compliance
- HackerRank maintains SOC 2 Type II certification.
- GDPR compliant — data processing agreements available on request.
- For infosec questionnaires or vendor security assessments, contact your account manager or submit a request through the HackerRank support portal.
- HackerRank does not fill in third-party security forms directly. Instead, they provide their security documentation package.

## GDPR and NYC AI Laws
- HackerRank provides GDPR-compliant data handling.
- Candidates can request data deletion.
- NYC AI Law compliance features available for bias auditing in assessments.

## Insights
- Dashboard showing hiring pipeline metrics.
- Test completion rates, candidate scores distribution, time-to-hire analytics.
- Export reports as CSV.
