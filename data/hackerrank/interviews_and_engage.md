# HackerRank Interviews & Engage

## Interviews

### Getting Started with HackerRank Interviews
HackerRank Interviews is a technical interview platform with a collaborative code editor, whiteboard, and video chat.

### Interview Settings
- **Inactivity timeout**: Interviews have configurable inactivity timeouts. By default, if a participant is inactive for a period, they may be removed from the session. Inactivity settings can be configured differently for candidates and interviewers.
- To request changes to inactivity timeout settings, contact HackerRank support or your account administrator.
- The inactivity timer tracks mouse and keyboard activity within the HackerRank interview window. Screen sharing in external tools does not count as activity in the HackerRank platform.

### Manage Interviews
- Schedule interviews through the platform or via ATS integration.
- Add interviewers and set up interview templates.
- Configure coding environments and question sets.

### Interview Integrity
- Plagiarism detection for interview submissions.
- Session recording (if enabled).

## HackerRank Engage (Mock Interviews)
HackerRank Engage is a product for mock interviews and practice.

### Mock Interviews
- Practice coding interviews with AI-powered feedback.
- Mock interviews simulate real interview conditions.
- Available as part of certain HackerRank plans.

### Billing for Engage
- Engage is a separate product from HackerRank for Work.
- Billing inquiries for Engage should be directed to HackerRank support.
- Refund requests for mock interviews should be submitted through HackerRank support.
- HackerRank evaluates refund requests on a case-by-case basis.

## SkillUp
HackerRank SkillUp provides learning paths and certifications for developers.

### Certifications
- Complete assessments to earn certificates.
- Certificates display your name as registered on your HackerRank profile.
- To update the name on a certificate, update your profile name first, then re-generate the certificate.
- If you have already completed the assessment, contact HackerRank support for certificate name corrections.

### Practice and Challenges
- Solve coding challenges across various domains.
- Track progress on learning paths.

## Resume Builder
HackerRank does not offer a resume builder feature. This is not part of the HackerRank platform. If you are looking for resume building tools, please use dedicated resume building services.

## Community
HackerRank Community is the platform for developers to practice coding, participate in contests, and earn badges.
- Community accounts are separate from HackerRank for Work accounts.
- Community users can delete their accounts through Settings > Delete Account.

## General Help

### Payment and Billing
- For payment issues with HackerRank subscriptions, contact support with your order ID or transaction reference.
- HackerRank uses Stripe for payment processing.
- Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis.

### Assessment Rescheduling
- HackerRank does not directly reschedule assessments for candidates.
- Assessment scheduling is managed by the hiring company that sent the invitation.
- Candidates should contact the recruiter or hiring company directly to request rescheduling.
- HackerRank support cannot modify test invitations or deadlines on behalf of candidates.

### Test Score Disputes
- HackerRank does not mediate between candidates and hiring companies regarding test scores.
- Test scoring is automated based on test case results.
- If a candidate believes there is a technical issue with grading, they can report it, but score adjustments are at the discretion of the test creator (hiring company).
- HackerRank cannot influence hiring decisions.

### Submissions Not Working
If submissions are not working:
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Clear browser cache and cookies.
3. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended).
4. Disable browser extensions.
5. Check if there are any active incidents on HackerRank status page.
6. If the issue persists, contact the test administrator or HackerRank support with details about the error.

### Apply Tab Not Visible
The Apply tab on HackerRank is part of the community/jobs feature. If you cannot see it:
1. Make sure you are logged into your community account.
2. Check if the feature is available in your region.
3. Clear browser cache.
4. If issue persists, contact HackerRank support.
